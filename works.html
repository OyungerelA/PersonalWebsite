Named "Filming a Film", this film was shot and produced in 30 minutes along with three other group members. As the name suggests, it depicts a team's journey of brainstorming and planning what to do for their 30-Minute film. Recorded on Zoom and edited on iMovie, the video exists on a webpage made with HTML, CSS and a tiny bit of Javascript.
